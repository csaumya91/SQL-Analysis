# SQL Analysis Summary

## Regional Revenue Concentration: Revenue contribution is different across markets, indicating dependency and potential for expansion opportunity in underpenetrated markets.This analysis supports region specific pricing adjustments and focus.

## Seasonality: Shows which months drive highest revenues. This analysis can help in maximising revenue during peak season and pricing strategy can be modified during lower revenue cycles.

## Customer Revenue Distribution: Customers contribute disproportionately high revenue share, suggesting enterprise retention strategies should be prioritized. Customer-level revenue clustering highlights importance of protecting enterprise accounts along with diversification to reduce dependency.

## Repeat Purchase Behavior: Customers with multiple transactions represent strong retention segments suitable for pricing-based loyalty strategies. Cohort targeting and bundling offers to repeat customers can result in higher AOV.

## Product level revenue drivers: Top performing SKUs indicate pricing leverage opportunities and revenue dependency. Helps in prioritizing any big-ticket items or low selling SKUs.
